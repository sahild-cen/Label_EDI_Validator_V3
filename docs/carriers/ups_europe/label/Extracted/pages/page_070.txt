UPS Manifest Summary (cont.)
Account Number, Shipping Record Number, Report and Collection Date Section
This section starts on the same line as the Company name, but on the right hand side of the page. The end of 
each line will be right-justified. The titles of UPS Account Number and Shipping Record Number are 12 pt. bold. 
All other titles/text are 10 pt. bold, unless otherwise indicated.
• Line 1 = 
The text “UPS Account Number:” followed by the Account Number in 12 pt. bold, uppercase
• Line 2 = 
The text “Shipping Record Number:” followed by the Shipping Record Number in 12 pt. 
• Line 3 = 
The text “Report Print Date:” followed by the date in the following format: DD/MMM/YYYY
There must be spaces before Line 4. Line 4 must print immediately above the separator line on the same line 
as the telephone number.
• Line 4 = The text “Collection Date:” followed by the collection date in the same format as Line 3.
• For Example: 
UPS Account Number:	
                         1X2X3X 	
   
Shipping Record Number:	
       9373309024
Report Print Date:	
                         27/JAN/2018
Collection Date:	
                             27/JAN/2018
SEPARATOR LINE
• Font size = 1pt, bold
