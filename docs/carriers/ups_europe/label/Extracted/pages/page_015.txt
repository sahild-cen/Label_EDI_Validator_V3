TRACKING BARCODE QUIET ZONES

Data Content
Positions	
1-2

• 
Minimum quiet zone top and bottom = 0.0625 
inches
Positions	
3 - 8	
=	 UPS Account Number
Positions	 9 -10	
=	 Service Indicator
• 
Minimum quiet zone left and right = 10 times the 
X-dimension
Positions	 11 -17 	
=	 Reference Number
Position	
18	
=	 Check Digit
TRACKING BARCODE FORMAT
• 
For Example:  1Z1X2X3XA112345671
• 

Left-justified, allowing for left quiet zone
• 
Spaces are not encoded in the barcode.
TRACKING BARCODE DIMENSIONS
• 
All alpha characters must be uppercase.
• 

Height (Minimum) = 0.7 inches
• 
Width = Variable
• 

X-Dimensions = 15 -21 mil
• 
Alpha characters may only appear in the 
1Z Data Identifier, the account number or 
the service indicator fields.
TRACKING BARCODE SPECIFICATIONS
• 

See Appendix B for Code 128 specifications.
• 
The reference number is determined by the shipper 
or shipping system and must be carefully controlled 
to ensure each package is unique.
• 
The entire 18 digit tracking number must remain 
unique for one year.
