UPS Smart Label™ Design 
Introduction
Enabling Timely Deliveries and Accurate Information
UPS continues to apply sophisticated technology to 
computer-generated labels. The latest innovations to the 
UPS Smart Label™ design provides prompt deliveries, more 
accurate routing and 24/7 access to up-to-the-minute tracking 
and delivery information for all packages and shipments.
UPS aims to provide timely, accurate delivery information. 
By continuously investing in technology, UPS ensures that 
the most up-to-date routing and tracking methods are 
used as packages move from one location to another. These 
advancements in technology helps UPS meet customer's 
transportation needs.
As soon as UPS scans the label, the physical (your package 
and the UPS delivery system) joins the virtual (the information 
about it).
The UPS Smart Label™ print contains five key data elements 
that enables it to be processed efficiently by the technologies 
used in the delivery network. The elements are:
The foundation of routing, tracking and information 
management at UPS is the automated scanning system that 
reads barcodes on each package. Every barcode uniquely 
identifies each individual package and relays information 
about that package’s movement until it reaches its final 
destination.
1. MaxiCode™ Symbology - contains the shipment detail.
2. Tracking Number Barcode - uniquely identifies your 
package in the UPS network.
3. UPS Routing Code: - identifies the UPS delivery facility.
4. Postal Barcode - contains the receiver’s postal code.
5. Service Icon - identifies the service you selected.
When modified by the shipper, barcodes provide added 
benefits, such as aggregating inventory SKUs, prices and 
purchase order numbers with UPS required shipment 
information. Whether uploaded via UPS shipping systems or 
UPS Ready™ vendor shipping systems, shippers receive the 
added benefit of linking their internal information with UPS’s 
scan information.
