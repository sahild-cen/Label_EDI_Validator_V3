Label Format Requirements
UPS Carbon Neutral
UPS Carbon Neutral
UPS has invested in alternative fuel and hybrid ve­
hicles; utilizes solar and wind energy; and works to 
maximize efficiencies throughout the delivery pro­
cess in an effort to minimize it’s  carbon footprint.
If space permits, the UPS Carbon Neutral shipment 
logo must print at the bottom left of the label.  Text 
must print if there is not enough room for the logo.  
The logo takes up around three lines of text in the 
bottom left section of the label.  Below are the 
height and width dimensions.
LOGO DIMENSIONS
• Height = 0.3 inches
• Width = 0.6 inches
NOTE:  The logo may be obtained from your UPS 
sales resource.
TEXT REQUIREMENT
If there is not enough room to print the logo, the 
following text may print:
• Text = UPS CARBON NEUTRAL SHIPMENT
• Font Size = 10 pt. bold
