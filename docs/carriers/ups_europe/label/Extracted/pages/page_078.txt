Method
Conversion from the 1Z Service Level Indicator 
value to its numeric equivalent is accomplished 
by taking the assigned decimal value of the first 
character and adding it to the assigned decimal 
value of the second character. To convert from 
three-character numeric to the 1Z Service Level 
Indicator, the reverse is done.
