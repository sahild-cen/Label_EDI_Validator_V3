ACCEPTABLE RANGES AND TOLERANCES
• 
Minimum quiet zone top and bottom = 1⁄
16 inch
• 
Minimum ANSI Grade B
• 

Narrow Width (X) = 18 mil +/– 3 mil for each bar 
and space (X dimensions must equal or fall between 
15 and 21 mil.)
• 


Bar and Space Tolerance (b/s) = + (21 – X) 
or – (X – 5) mil (Any deviation of the 4 individual 
widths must not fall outside the specified ranges.)
• 

Edge-to-Edge Tolerance (e) = +/– 0.20X mil
(The sum of any adjacent bar space widths must 
be within 0.20X mil tolerance from the nominal 
edge-to-edge width.)
• 

Pitch Tolerance (p) = +/– 0.20X mil
(The width of any character must be within 0.20X mil 
tolerance from the nominal character width.)
