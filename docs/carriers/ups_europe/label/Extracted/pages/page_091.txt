Base 26 Calculation Example 2:
	 1. 
Using the first 1Z tracking number in the shipment:
1Z 123 X56  Y6  4400 0050
	 2. 
Exclude the Data Identifier  “1Z”:
_ _ 123 X56  Y6  4400 0050
	 3. 
Exclude the shipper number:
_ _  _ _ _  _ _ _  Y6  4400 0050
	 4. 
Exclude the service level indicator and check digit:
_ _  _ _ _  _ _ _  _ _  4400 005_
	 5. 
Calculate	
4400 005 : 264 = 9.628
	 6. 
Calculate	
(4400 005 — (9*264)) : 263 = 16.341
	 7. 
Calculate	
(4400 005 — (9*264) — (16*263)) : 262 = 8.883
	 8. 
Calculate	
(4400 005 — (9*264) — (16*263) — (8*262)) : 26 = 22.961
	 9. 
Calculate	
4400 005 — (9*264) — (16*263) — (8*262) — (22*26) = 25.000
