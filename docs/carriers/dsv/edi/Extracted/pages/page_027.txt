General Notes
Codes ZXD,ZXE and ZXP are targeting Courier and the DSV Xpress application. EDI for this application is work in progress so not
generally available at this time.
Observe that codes for Time Option value is always an additional service and is therefore never sent as the first service requirement.
It is always presented after the main service has been informed. And only related to courier products.
