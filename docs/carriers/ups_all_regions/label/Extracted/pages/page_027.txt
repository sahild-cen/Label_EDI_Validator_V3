 Label Format Requirements
Sub-Pro Number Barcode Block
Legal Text Block
The Legal Text Block contains the International 
Shipping Notice and is located at the bottom of the 
label.  
HUMAN READABLE SHIPMENT ID NUMBER
The Sub-Pro Number human readable interpretation 
will appear immediately below the Consolidated 
Movement Block. The Sub-Pro Number must be 
formatted as shown in the examples below.
Please refer to the International Shipping Notice text 
in the UPS Guide to Labeling.
