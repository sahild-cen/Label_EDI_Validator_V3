Label Format Requirements
When shipping ISC, the following text must print 
centered in the Additional Routing Instructions block 
of the label immediately below the documentation 
indicators.
• 
Routing Instruction = ISC
• 
Font Size                       = 10 pt. bold
International Special Commodities (ISC) service is 
offered by contract only.  An ISC contract allows the 
shipper to send certain restricted articles such as:  
alcoholic beverages, perishables, seeds, tobacco and 
plants.  For ISC shipments, the shipping label must 
contain additional information to meet regulatory 
requirements and assist UPS in processing the 
shipment.
