Consolidated Movement Block
The Consolidated Movement Block must appear below 
the Ship To address separated by a horizontal line. This 
block contains port codes and service level separated by 
a vertical line.
Port codes for air movements will be three characters. 
Port codes for ocean movements 
will be five characters.
