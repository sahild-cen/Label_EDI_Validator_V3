Introduction
International Documentation Indicator
UPS allows customers to upload images of their 
international shipping documents.  The forms 
include the following:
• 
 Commercial Invoice
• 
 Shipper Export Declaration
• 
 US Certificate of Origin
When uploading images of international forms, a  
special International Documentation Indicator must 
be printed on the label.  The indicator will alert UPS 
Operations that images for the shipment have been 
uploaded and paper forms may not be attached to 
the package.  The indicator is:
• 
 NAFTA Certificate of Origin
• 
EDI-IDIS
• 
 Packing List
• 
Font Size = 16 pt. bold
• 
