UPS® Premier is a contractual service by which 
customers can easily ship complex, sensitive packages 
(e.g., healthcare and pharmaceutical items) with the 
assurance that these packages will be treated with 
the highest level of care.  UPS® Premier customers 
will have the flexibility to choose not only from 
multiple levels (Silver, Gold, & Platinum) to best 
suit their shipping needs, but also from numerous 
special handling instructions to ensure the integrity 
of their shipments by prompting rapid response time 
should such packages become distressed.  The Radio 
Frequency Identification (RFID) labels for UPS Premier 
Silver Packages and the Mesh Identifier for UPS Premier 
Gold and Platinum Packages contribute to this rapid 
response.  UPS® Premier provides customers a high 
level of visibility into their critical shipments, resulting 
in greater reliability and confidence in UPS service.
Label Format Requirements
The following text must print in the Package 
Information Block of a UPS® Premier label for all origins 
and destinations. The required text depends on the 
UPS® Premier service option selected. 
UPS® Premier Silver:
• Data Content  =  SLV
• Font Size           =  16 pt. bold
UPS® Premier Gold:
• Data Content  =  GLD
• Font Size           =  16 pt. bold
UPS® Premier Platinum:
• Data Content  =  PLT
• Font Size           =  16 pt. bold
