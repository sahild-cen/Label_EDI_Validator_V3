UPS offers the ability to ship export-controlled 
articles that require State Department License (SDL) 
or license Exemption. For SDL or SDL exemption 
shipments, the shipping label must contain 
additional information to assist UPS in processing 
your shipment.
For SDL shipments, sent via Air Freight the "SDL" 
indication and the ITN must print in the routing 
section of the label.

International Documentation Indicators
When shipping export-controlled articles 
that require SDL or license exemption special 
International Documentation Indicators must be 
printed on the label to alert UPS Operations about 
the type of shipment.  For all State Department 
Licensed or Exempted shipments the indicator on 
the label should be:
