# Field: governmental_procedures

## Display Name
Governmental Procedures (Import/Export)

## Field Description
A segment (SG33 - GOR) to indicate applicable governmental procedures related to import, transport of the goods. Appears at both shipment and invoice levels.

## Format & Validation Rules
- **Data Type:** alphanumeric
- **Length:** variable
- **Pattern/Regex:** Not specified in spec
- **Allowed Values:** Not restricted
- **Required:** conditional — 1 occurrence at SG33

## Examples from Spec
No examples in spec.

## Position on Label
Not specified in spec.

## Edge Cases & Notes
At invoice level, GOR indicates the Document Function, and is accompanied by FTX for Trading Transaction Type information.

## Claude Confidence
MEDIUM — Described at both shipment and invoice levels.
