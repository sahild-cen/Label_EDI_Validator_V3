Appendix D – Message structure and content for Loose BBX shipments 
and Sample Message 
Break Bulk Express (BBX) is a DHL Express service which streamlines destination Customs procedures, 
minimizing Customs lodgment fees charged. It includes the submission of shipment data records for a 
“Mother” shipment and a number of corresponding “Baby” shipments. 
Consolidated BBX is where the Baby shipments are packed together, e.g. on pallets, forming a smaller 
number of pieces of the Mother shipment (e.g. 1 pallet = 1 Mother shipment piece) 
Loose BBX is where the BBX service is used but shipments are not packed together (e.g. not palletized).
