Example:  
MEA+WT+AAE+KGM:12’ 
MEA+VOL+AAW+MTQ:45.000’ 
 
Note: 
The segment will occur twice. The first occurrence is reserved for the piece weight and 
the second for the piece volume. 
 
Note 6311:  
Use: ‘WT’ – Weight. 
         ‘VOL’ - Volume. 
 
Note C108/4440: This segment with element 6311 = VOL, becomes mandatory if no dimensions of the          
piece are available. 
 
Note 6313:  
Use: ‘AAE’ - Piece gross weight. 
        ‘AAW’ - Gross volume. 
 
Note 6411:  
Confirm with DHL Express which of the following units can be supported in the shipment 
origin country: 
For WT ‘KGM or ‘LBR’ 
For VOL MTQ, CMQ or INQ 
 
Note 6314:  
• The gross weight and volume of the item are allowed with a maximum of 3 decimals. 
• Decimal sign may be point or comma depending of what specified in segment UNA.
