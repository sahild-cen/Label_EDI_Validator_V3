Units of Measure 
The following codes are valid for unit of measurement for weight, volume and dimensions 
Weight 
Default = KGM (Kilograms) 
Also acceptable: LBR (Pounds) 
Volume 
Default = MTQ (Cubic Meters) 
Also acceptable: CMQ (Cubic Centimeters), INQ (Cubic Inches) 
Dimensions (Length, Width, Height) 
Default = CMT (Centimeters) 
Also acceptable: MTR (Meters), INH (Inches)
