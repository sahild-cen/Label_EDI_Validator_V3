Note: 
Segment group 31 is mandatory to indicate freight costs payment and also to register the 
agreement between the Seller and the Buyer (Incoterms). On conditional base, product 
features can be indicated. If you wish to have more information about the product 
features and availability please contact your Sales representative. 
 
Note 4055:
