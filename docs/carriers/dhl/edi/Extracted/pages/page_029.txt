Note: 
This segment is used to indicate total gross weight for the entire shipment, total number 
of pieces in the shipment and total volume of the shipment. 
The total volume is required for a shipment with destination outside the Shipper country. 
It can be provided here or the dimensions per item can be provided later in the GID 
segment. 
The maximum of pieces cannot exceed 999 in one shipment. 
 
Note 6069: 
Use: 
‘7’ - Gross Weight. In 6069, the value may be specified with maximum 1 decimal. 
‘11’ - Total number of Packages. In 6069 the value may be specified with maximum 3 
decimals. 
‘15’ - Cube, Volume. 
 
Decimal sign may be point or comma depending of what has been specified in the UNA 
segment. 
 
Note 6411: 
Confirm with DHL Express which of the following units can be supported in the shipment 
origin country: 
With ‘7’ in 6069 ‘KGM or ‘LBR’ 
With ‘15’ in 6069 MTQ, CMQ or INQ 
Element 6411 is not used with ‘8’ in 6069
