Qualifier ACL is used for bulk shipments. Break Bulk (baby) shipments come from the 
same shipper, but can have different receivers. They are grouped to 1 Break Bulk 
shipment, to accommodate the customs Clearance of the “Bulk Shipment” only and not 
all of the associated “Baby Shipments”. The baby shipments must have the Product 
code: Break bulk (BBE or BBX) assigned to it. The Break Bulk shipment travels under the 
standard Product code (like ESI or ESU). This Break Bulk shipment is just an aggregation 
of the “Baby shipments” and travels FOC (Free of Charge) over the network. It terminates 
after Customs clearance in the inbound country has taken place. For more information 
about Break Bulk Express, please contact your Sales Representative.
