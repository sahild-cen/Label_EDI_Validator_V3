Saturday 
Delivery 
AA 
050 
Holidays 
Weekends & 
At Shipper's request, the international delivery of a 
shipment/piece on a Saturday, to selected 
postal/zip code areas that have the capability, in 
countries where Saturday is not a normal working 
day. For more information about this service and 
availability, please contact your Sales 
representative. 
Supplementary 
Insurance 
II 
101 
Insurance Services 
At Shipper's request, the provision at individual 
shipment level, of declared value coverage above 
Standard Liability, for the amount necessary to 
repair or replace a shipment/piece in the event of 
physical loss or damage.  
Called Shipment Value Protection in the US/CA. 
For more information about this service and 
availability, please contact your Sales 
representative. 
Cash on 
Delivery 
KB 
100 
Billing Instructions 
By contractual agreement with the Shipper, the 
delivery of a shipment/piece only against payment 
of the goods value indicated by the Shipper on the 
waybill (below a certain maximum amount). 
Procedures and means of payment to the Courier 
depend on local conditions and practices (cash, 
bank guaranteed cheques, or credit card).  
Available only with domestic products. 
Only a Shipper with an account and who 
contractually guarantees the amount of such 
shipments can use this service. 
For more information about this service and 
availability, please contact your Sales 
representative.
