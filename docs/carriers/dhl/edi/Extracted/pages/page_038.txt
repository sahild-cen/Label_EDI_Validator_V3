Note: 
This segment represents control totals within respective Shipment. 
 
For “LBBX Customer Controlled solution” purposes - this segment should be sent within 
the each ‘baby’ shipment. 
 
Note 6069: 
Use: 
‘56’ – for LBBX purposes: Total number of transport units (number of mother pieces = 
sum of all baby pieces – optional.) 
 
Decimal sign may be point or comma depending of what has been specified in the UNA 
segment. 
 
Note 6411: 
Use: 
Element 6411 is not used with ‘56’ in 6069.
