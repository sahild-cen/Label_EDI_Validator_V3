Note 2005:  
Use: 
 ‘191’ – Expected/Estimated Delivery date/time 
 
Note 2380: 
With ‘191’ in preceding element, this is the Estimated Delivery or POD date time. 
Note 2379: 
Use: 
‘203’ for the date time mask CCYYMMDDHHMM 
‘102’ for the date mask CCYYMMDD
