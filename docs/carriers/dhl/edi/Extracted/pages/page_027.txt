Note 5025: 
Use: ‘43’ - Declared total customs value. 
 
Note 5004: 
This is a value represented for all shipment within the file. 
                          Rules for numeric value: 
▪ 
The maximum number of digit is 18 where the 2 decimals are included. 
▪ 
All amounts have to be specified with 2 decimals included, regardless currency. 
▪ 
Decimal sign may be point or comma depending of what has been specified in 
segment UNA. 
▪ 
COD value has to be given in the currency of the destination country. 
 
Note 6345: 
Currency codes are according to ISO 4217.  
This must be the currency of the shipper country except for Cash On Delivery (COD) 
where it will the currency of the destination country.
