8.1.7. 
  Considerations on 2D-enabled Transport Labels 
This section gives the recommended segment and font sizes for “2D-enabled Transport Labels” labels as they 
should be used on thermo printers where possible and other printer types when allowed.  
2D-enabled Labels may exist with the usual standard formats (8” or 6”) or new ones (e.g. customer-tailored, 
upon DHL EXPRESS approval and subject to a formal Global Label Certification).
