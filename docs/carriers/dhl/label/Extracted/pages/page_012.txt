4.4. Documentation-related Principles 
• 
A mandatory-marked field implies that the content is ALWAYS mandatory. This implies that 
its absence is Not acceptable because it impacts the quality of the label data/ info and thus of 
the associated shipment/ piece handling processes. 
• 
A Conditional-marked field implies that the content is mandatory in some cases (e.g. for 
a specific product) and optional in other cases. Further details accompany the specific 
fields 
• 
An Optional-marked field indicates that the content is not required, but may be added if 
data is available and brings value. Optional does not always imply that the field may 
remain empty. Further details accompany the specific fields and may require that for 
example they have to be populated with at least zeroes.
