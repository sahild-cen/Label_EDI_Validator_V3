6.5. Country-Facility ID 
 
The DHL Country Code and Facility Identifier for both Original and Destination locations will be displayed here, 
with the following format: 
 
• 
[ORIGIN COUNTRY CODE]-[ORIGIN SERVICE AREA CODE] [ORIGIN FACILITY CODE], in 
correspondence to the DHL Facility managing the Shipper’s GEO Area 
 
• 
[DESTINATION COUNTRY CODE]-[DESTINATION SERVICE AREA CODE] [DESTINATION 
FACILITY CODE], in correspondence to the definition applied to the corresponding Transport 
Label and to the DHL Facility managing the Consignee’s GEO Area
