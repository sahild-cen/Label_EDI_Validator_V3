2. Objective 
The objective of this Reference guide is to ensure all stakeholders: 
• 
recognize and understand the business requirements on data and information on the Label 
• 
(customers) recognize and understand the specifications, which must be adhered to and met, to be 
compliant with DHL EXPRESS’ Label Standards, before accepted utilization is possible 
• 
are able to identify issues and/or non-compliances with the Label and initiate mitigation actions
