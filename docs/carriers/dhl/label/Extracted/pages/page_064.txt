8.1.4. 
  Considerations on labels for 8” or A4 landscape print 
This section gives the recommended segment and font sizes for v2.8 labels as they should be used on 
thermo printers where possible. The length of 8” is equivalent to 20.3 cm which makes this a good fit for 
A4 or letter format sheets in landscape format. One sheet can contain two or (in the case of A4) three 
labels, or one label plus further information.
