Permanent Label Certification Identifier (mandatory) 
 
All Label Certification Identifiers are managed centrally, by the GCoCL team and follow a sequential scheme. 
There is a differentiation between permanently and temporary approved labels which still require some rework

5.2.3.2. Temporary Label Certification Identifier 
 
Temporary Label Certification Identifier Convention: 
A temporary certification can be used for a limited period but must be reviewed after 6 months to fully adhere 
to the DHL Express standards. It will follow a different naming convention starting with a fix suffix followed by a 
serial number and date. Once fully compliant, the temporary ID will be changed to a permanent ID  
 
 
The font size for the Certification Identifiers is of a 1.5mm font size or 5 points. 
 
5.2.4. 
Label Printing Date (mandatory) 
 
The Label Printing Date, in accordance with the ISO 8601 standard, follows (sequence) the identification of the 
Rendering Application and has the format yyyy-mm-dd5, where “YYYY” refers to the year, “mm” to the month 
and “dd” to the date. 
 
5 In the case of GLS it is based on the server’s date and time
