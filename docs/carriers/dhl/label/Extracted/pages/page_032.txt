5.11.4.1.1. Standard Location of Weight Symbols 
 
The new Weight Symbol shall be located in the current segment containing the Piece and Shipment Weights, 
on their left side.  
 
To ensure that the weight icon is easily visible in all distinct templates and does not overlap with other shipment 
fields, fine adjustments and variations of the Symbol locations are acceptable.  
 
Implementation of deviations from the Global SOP Standard Transport Label and respective Weight Symbol 
location require pre-approval from Global SOP or from the Global Competence Center for Labels and Barcodes.
