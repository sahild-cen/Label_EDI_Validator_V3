8.1.3. 
  Considerations on A6 or 6”x4” (inch) Labels 
The ESS Label v2.8 fits on an A6 label (14.85 cm) and on a 6”x4” (15.24 cm) label.  
However, the design has to be done very carefully, and the printing will not allow for huge tolerances. 
Users in the USA are generally recommended to use 6 ¾” x 4” labels.
