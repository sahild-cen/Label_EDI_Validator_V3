The Data Identifier preceding the Piece Identifier is NOT part of the Piece Identifier. It does not have 
to be considered when determining the Piece Identifier length. Two Piece Identifiers are identical if 
their only difference is a deviating data identifier (e.g. “J” versus “2J”).  
 
However, the Data Identifier always has to be printed on the label both in human readable and bar-
coded format. The bar-coded Data Identifier must not include the parentheses which are used for clarity 
only.  
(The bar-coded form of the data identifier and Piece Identifier is then “JJD01…….”. & the human 
readable form should be in the form “(J)JD01 …..”.)
