Check Digit Calculation (Modified MOD 10)
1. Exclude “1Z” Data Identifier from calculation
4. 
From left, add all even positions and multiply 
by two
2. 
Convert all alpha characters to their numeric 
equivalents (see cross-reference table below)
5. 
Add results of steps 3 and 4
3. 
From left, add all odd positions
6. 
Subtract result from next highest multiple of 10
7. 
The remainder is your check digit
